SolutionFactory

We offer high quality WordPress solutions.

All our WordPress solutions follow WordPress guidelines and programming best practices. In practice this leads to extendable, testable, flexible and reusable plugins.

Goal

Our goal is to create a repository if WordPress solutions done well. The problem with a lot of the WordPress ecosystem is that developers of plugins and solutions don't take into account best coding practices making most solutions a pain to work with at best.

Guidelines and best practices

All our projects are based on WordPress development best practices and general programming best practices. This ensures the quality of our solutions. This means a.o. that all our solutions are:

  • translation ready
  • extendable
  • easily customizable
  • testable (unit tests)
  • loosely coupled
  • namespaced (to prevent collisions with other tools)
  • OOP (Object Oriented)
  • high quality

Framework

We have created an open source and free (as in beer) plugin framework which we use in all our solutions. This framework is free for everyone to use to make your own great WordPress solutions!

Supported PHP versions

PHP started to become a proper OOP language starting with PHP 5.3. Because of this all our solutions have the minimum requirement of that version. Any PHP version starting at 5.3 (including even HHVM) is supported by all our solutions.
